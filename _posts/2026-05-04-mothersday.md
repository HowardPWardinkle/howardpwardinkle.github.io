## Idea

As usual, for the two May cards I use the same mechanism. See the [May](/2026/04/22/may.html) card for the design details.

## Differences

The mechanism are very similar. Both cards have four layers. The Mother's Day design is much more organic, topped off with a flower. The base of the card has pastel vines and the rest is colored cardstock. The original design had larger upper layers, but the text overlapped too much, so I had to shrink them.

![Top](/images/2605/mday-top.jpg){: width="{{ site.imageWidth }}" }
*Top view*{: .image-caption}

The layers have fewer supports making it bend, which gives it a bit more organic feel.
